Privacy Policy

Your trust is paramount. This policy outlines how Kredcommerce Systems Private Limited handles your data with utmost care and transparency.

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name, email, phone, PAN, Aadhaar, and other identification details.
  • Device Data: IP address, device type, OS, unique device identifiers, and app usage data.
  • Financial Information: Credit reports, bank account details, transaction history, and loan records.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process loan applications, verify identity, and assess eligibility.
  • To evaluate creditworthiness and share with partnered lenders.
  • To provide personalized customer support and improve services.
  • For internal analytics, product development, and platform enhancements.

Sharing of Data

We share your data strictly with:

  • Partner Banks and NBFCs: For loan facilitation and product offerings.
  • Credit Bureaus: For credit reporting and assessment, as per legal norms.
  • Regulatory Bodies: When legally mandated for compliance.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors under stringent confidentiality agreements for operational support.

Data Security

We employ robust encryption and industry-leading security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access. While no system is entirely invulnerable, we are committed to safeguarding your information and advise caution with sensitive details.

Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: Request to view or correct your personal data.
  • Deletion or Restriction: Request data deletion or processing restriction, adhering to legal mandates.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time, which may impact service availability.

Cookies & Tracking

We utilize cookies and similar technologies for analytics, content personalization, and service enhancement. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Our services are exclusively for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not intentionally collect data from minors. Any such data found will be promptly deleted.

Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Significant changes will be announced through platform notifications, email, or other relevant communication channels.
