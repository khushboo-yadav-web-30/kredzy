Find answers to common questions about Kredzy

Kredzy is a fintech platform that connects users with the right financial products, including government-backed loans, private loans, NBFC loans, and in-house financing. We simplify the lending process and promote financial literacy.

Kredzy is currently a financial technology platform that partners with banks, NBFCs, and other financial institutions. We are also in the process of establishing our own NBFC for direct lending.

Kredzy facilitates:
  • Government-backed loans (e.g., Mudra, Stand-Up India)
  • Personal and business loans
  • Loans from NBFCs
  • Self-NBFC loans (Kredzy lending in future)
